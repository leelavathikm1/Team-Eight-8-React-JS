Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams

Entry Darshan

The Special Entry Darshan(Seeghra Darshanam) is introduced on 21-Sep-2009 to provide quick Darshan to the Pilgrims. The cost of the Ticket is Rs.300/- per pilgrim with Single Laddu free of cost and booking is open round the clock. The Advance Booking of Special Entry Darshan is introduced w.e.f. 27.August.2014. Pilgrims can book Special Entry Darshan tickets for the current day 3 hours prior to Darshan Slot subject to availability of Quota. Pilgrims can make eHundi Offering while booking Darshan tickets. Pilgrims can avail the Advance Booking of Special Entry Darshan through Internet Booking(https://tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in), and Indian Post Offices.
